creamy chicken pesto pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound pasta (penne, fettuccine, or spaghetti)
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, oil-packed and chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta: Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Cook in a skillet over medium-high heat until cooked through. Let cool and shred.
  3. Make the sauce: In a large skillet, combine the pesto, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. Heat over medium heat until the sauce is creamy and heated through.
  4. Combine ingredients: Add the cooked pasta, shredded chicken, and sun-dried tomatoes to the sauce. Toss to coat.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately, topped with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips:

  • For a spicier dish, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • You can substitute sun-dried tomatoes with fresh basil.
  • Serve with a side salad or garlic bread.

Bow Tie Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound bow tie pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta: Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and let cool.
  2. Combine vegetables: In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, and dill.
  3. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss the salad: Add the cooked pasta to the bowl with the vegetables. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately or chill for later.

Tips:

  • For a spicier salad, add a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • You can add other vegetables to this salad, such as bell peppers, carrots, or celery.
  • Serve with grilled chicken or fish for a complete meal.

Spaghetti and Meat Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pound spaghetti

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta: Cook the spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Brown the meat: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and pork over medium-high heat. Drain off any excess grease.
  3. Sauté the onions and garlic: Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and sauté until softened.
  4. Add the sauce ingredients: Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, beef broth, red wine, olive oil, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Simmer the sauce: Reduce heat to low and simmer for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours, to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Combine pasta and sauce: Toss the cooked spaghetti with the meat sauce.
  7. Serve: Serve immediately, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips:

  • For a spicier sauce, add a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • You can substitute ground beef and pork with Italian sausage.
  • Serve with a side salad or garlic bread.

Tomato Spinach Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound pasta (penne, fettuccine, or spaghetti)
  • 1 jar (15 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 1 bunch fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta: Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Cook in a skillet over medium-high heat until cooked through. Let cool and shred.
  3. Heat the sauce: In a large skillet, heat the marinara sauce over medium heat.
  4. Add the spinach: Add the chopped spinach to the heated sauce and cook until wilted.
  5. Combine ingredients: Add the cooked pasta, shredded chicken, and Parmesan cheese to the sauce. Toss to coat.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately, topped with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips:

  • For a spicier dish, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the marinara sauce.
  • You can substitute fresh spinach with frozen spinach.
  • Serve with a side salad or garlic bread.

Vegan Pasta alla Vodka

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pasta (penne, fettuccine, or spaghetti)
  • 1 jar (15 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup vodka
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (or a vegan alternative)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (or a vegan alternative)
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta: Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the sauce: In a large skillet, combine the marinara sauce, vodka, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, oregano, and basil. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Simmer the sauce: Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-10 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Combine pasta and sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately, topped with additional Parmesan cheese (or a vegan alternative) if desired.

Tips:

  • For a spicier dish, add a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.
  • You can substitute heavy cream with a vegan alternative like coconut milk or cashew cream.
  • Serve with a side salad or garlic bread.
