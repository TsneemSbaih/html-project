Beetroot salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 large beetroot, peeled and diced
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Peel and dice the beetroot and carrot. Thinly slice the red onion.
  2. Combine vegetables: In a large bowl, combine the beetroot, carrot, red onion, parsley, and dill.
  3. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss the salad: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately or chill for later.

Tips:

  • For a sweeter salad, add more honey.
  • For a spicier salad, add a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • Serve with grilled chicken or fish for a complete meal.

Kale salad

Ingredients:

For the salad:

  • 1 bunch kale, stems removed and roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts

For the dressing:

  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, water, salt, and pepper.
  2. Prepare the kale: Massage the kale with your hands to break down the tough fibers.
  3. Combine ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the kale, cherry tomatoes, red onion, feta cheese, and pine nuts.
  4. Toss with dressing: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately or chill for later.

Tips:

  • For a spicier salad, add a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • You can substitute feta cheese with goat cheese or Parmesan cheese.
  • For a more crunchy salad, add toasted almonds or sunflower seeds.

Fattoush salad

Ingredients:

For the salad:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 pita bread, cut into small pieces and toasted

For the dressing:

  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Combine vegetables: In a large bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, cucumber, tomato, parsley, mint, and red onion.
  3. Toss with dressing: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Add pita bread: Add the toasted pita bread to the salad and toss again.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately or chill for later.

Tips:

  • For a spicier salad, add a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • You can substitute romaine lettuce with other types of lettuce, such as iceberg or spinach.
  • For a more flavorful salad, marinate the cucumber and red onion in lemon juice and olive oil for 30 minutes before adding them to the salad.

Halloumi salad

Ingredients:

For the salad:

  • 1 block halloumi cheese, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint

For the dressing:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Grill the vegetables: Grill the red bell pepper, zucchini, and yellow squash until tender. Let cool.
  2. Grill the halloumi: Grill the halloumi cheese until golden brown and slightly crispy. Let cool.
  3. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the grilled vegetables, cherry tomatoes, red onion, parsley, mint, and grilled halloumi cheese.
  5. Toss with dressing: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately or chill for later.

Tips:

  • For a spicier salad, add a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • You can substitute halloumi cheese with feta cheese or goat cheese.
  • Serve with grilled chicken or fish for a complete meal.
