Cream of Mushroom Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the vegetables: Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the mushrooms: Add the mushrooms to the pot and cook until softened and browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the broth and cream: Stir in the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Simmer: Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the soup has thickened.
  5. Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Optional additions:

  • Cheese: Add grated Parmesan cheese or shredded Swiss cheese for a more creamy and flavorful soup.
  • Herbs: Add fresh thyme or parsley for a touch of herbaceous flavor.
  • Bread: Serve with crusty bread for dipping.

Cream Of Onion Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the onions: Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté until softened and caramelized, about 20-30 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic: Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute more.
  3. Add the broth and cream: Stir in the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Simmer: Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the soup has thickened.
  5. Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Optional additions:

  • Cheese: Top with grated Gruyère or Swiss cheese.
  • Croutons: Add croutons for a crunchy texture.
  • Bread: Serve with crusty bread for dipping.

Potato Leek Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 leek, white part only, thinly sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the leek: Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the leek and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the potatoes: Add the potatoes to the pot and cook until softened, about 10-15 minutes.
  3. Add the broth and cream: Stir in the chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Simmer: Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the soup has thickened.
  5. Blend: Use an immersion blender or transfer the soup to a blender and puree until smooth.
  6. Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste

Optional additions:

  • Cheese: Top with grated Parmesan or cheddar cheese.
  • Herbs: Add fresh thyme or chives for a touch of flavor.
  • Bread: Serve with crusty bread for dipping.

Wonton Soup

Ingredients:

For the wonton wrappers

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup hot water

For the wonton filling:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1/4 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1/4 cup chopped water chestnuts
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

For the soup:

  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Make the wonton wrappers:
    • In a small bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the hot water and stir until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth.
    • Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.
    • Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/8 inch thickness. Cut the dough into squares.
    • Place a spoonful of the wonton filling in the center of each square. Fold the dough over the filling and pinch the edges together to seal.
  2. Make the soup:
    • In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a boil.
    • Add the wontons to the boiling broth and cook for 3-5 minutes, or until the wontons float to the surface.
    • Stir in the green onions, soy sauce, and sesame oil.

  • You can also use ground chicken or shrimp for the wonton filling.
  • For a vegetarian option, use a mixture of tofu, vegetables, and seasonings for the filling.
  • Serve the wonton soup with a side of soy sauce and rice vinegar for dipping.

Spicy Sausage and Tortellini Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) chicken broth
  • 1 pound frozen cheese tortellini
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Brown the sausage: In a large pot, brown the sausage over medium heat. Break up the sausage with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic: Add the onion and garlic to the pot and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the tomatoes and broth: Stir in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the tortellini: Add the frozen tortellini to the pot and cook according to package directions.
  5. Add the cream and seasonings: Stir in the heavy cream, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer: Simmer for 5-10 minutes, or until the flavors have combined.

Tips:

  • For a spicier soup, add more red pepper flakes.
  • You can use different types of tortellini, such as spinach and ricotta or cheese and mushroom.
  • Serve with a side of crusty bread.
